03 — Solution

Core Features

PillEd integrates six critical capabilities into a single app — a combination no competitor currently offers.

📷

OCR Medication Scan

Point your camera at any medication to instantly identify it. Our image recognition engine matches pills against a comprehensive drug database.

⚠️

Drug Interaction Alerts

Automatic drug-drug interaction checking with risk level classification (High/Medium/Low). Real-time contraindication warnings based on user profile.

⏰

Smart Scheduling

Personalized dosing schedules with push notifications. Snooze support, dose tracking history, and adherence analytics built in.

📖

Digestible Drug Info

Medical jargon translated into plain, understandable language. Dosage, storage, side effects, and precautions presented clearly.

💰

Price Comparison

Compare prices across retail pharmacy partners. Add medications to a wishlist and purchase directly through in-app integrations.

👤

Personalized Profile

Age, gender, weight, pregnancy status, allergies — all factored into personalized contraindication alerts and recommendations.

07 — Go-to-Market Strategy

Phased Market Entry

A B2B-first approach to build medical credibility before scaling to consumers.

Q1 2023

Hospital Partnerships

Partner with hospitals to optimize medication reconciliation and build legitimacy within the medical community.

Q2 2023

Clinical Channel Expansion

Partner with skilled nursing facilities, obstetricians, and geriatricians to promote app downloads. Serving busy outpatient clinics and under-resourced nursing facilities.

Q4 2023

B2B Healthcare Retail

B2B partnerships with healthcare retail to provide promotion coupons from pharmaceutical companies to attract new customers.

Q1 2025

Product Launch

Full public launch with established medical community trust, retail partnerships, and a validated product-market fit.
